Preface

Inspired by the immersive childlike atmosphere of Ghibli Studios that grants its audience the luxury of escapism, I find myself with an urge to create a character with a personality of charm and courage, and as she leaps into the embrace of her destiny, she will also assist me in introducing my world to her readers.

Similar to my previous work, Little Aesena is a First-Person Point-Of-Thought story in Present-Tense, an experimental writing style that I fancy. I wished to properly capture how people regard their surroundings, to sufficiently portray the thoughts and descriptions they can have in their minds.

At the same time, I entertain the idea of a character that will grow with her readers through real-time. With children in the age range of 8 to 15 as her target audience, I hope to live up to such a vision.

I am here, to make something beautiful. Will you see it through with me?

Breaking The Fourth Wall

'right, so let us give it name: this world that Little Aesena takes place in is called 'The World Above'. And let us call our world, this one we live in now: 'The World We Know'. Now there are many similarities between both worlds, such as the sharing of the dominant language of English(called 'Anglish' in The World Above) and being in the modern era.

There are slight differences, however, like calendar and clock systems. The World Above has sixty hours per day and eight days per week and thirty days per month. The seconds tick a tiny bit faster, but the days there are doubtlessly longer than the days in The World We Know. Also, it has multiple suns and moons; then here comes the more notable distinctions: the abundance of humanoid races apart from Men and, of course, the existence of Magic.
